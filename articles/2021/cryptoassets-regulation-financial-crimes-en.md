# Cryptoassets: Regulation and Emerging Challenges in Combating Financial Crimes

**Author:** Jaison Sfogia Ricardo  
**Published in:** Revista da UNAERP  
**Year:** 2021  
**Language:** English  

## Abstract
This article analyzes the phenomenon of virtual assets in light of the establishment of legal norms to regulate the circulation of cryptoassets and to combat all illicit activities related to these assets. Accordingly, it describes the mechanisms of their operation and the emerging problems of a legal nature, considering that global digitalization requires new requirements without which it will not be possible to achieve legal regulation appropriate to the digital age. The research is based on the analytical method and was conducted using literary and normative sources. The novelty of this study lies in the need to align legal and information systems with the rapid process of digitalization in society. In this regard, it is observed that the lack of legal regulation and technical resources makes new digital solutions attractive for illicit activities, and the longer it takes to resolve regulatory issues, the greater the risks associated with the use of virtual assets will become.
